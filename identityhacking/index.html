Identity Hacking project was inspired when I saw my friend tried to unlock his laptop by using the webcam feature to read his face instead of using type password. My question is what if someone tries to access to your laptop or your personal devices, such as smartphone and tablet, to steal your private informations by scanning your face while you are sleeping? or using you photo or video to access to your personal devices. To avoid the facial recognition of computer and orther devices, I design a website where you can design your own facial identity which only you know about it. Enjoy!
